# Atlas AI Provider Disclosure

Status: Draft for legal/commercial review
Date: 2026-06-08
Owner system: GitHub
Related issue: #4

## Purpose

This document controls public wording for third-party AI platform references in Atlas AI website copy, pricing copy, onboarding copy and Trust Center content.

## Disclosure principle

Atlas AI provides a governed solution layer: workflow design, configuration, onboarding, documentation, support, governance and delivery controls. Atlas AI does not present itself as a proprietary foundation-model provider or official partner of any AI platform unless a separate contract and legal review confirms that status.

## Provider references

Provider names may be used as explanatory platform lanes where relevant. They must not be used as Atlas-owned product names or imply endorsement, resale rights, official partnership or guaranteed platform availability.

Examples of allowed descriptive references:

- OpenAI / ChatGPT platform lane
- Microsoft 365 Copilot platform lane
- Google Gemini platform lane
- Anthropic Claude platform lane
- Perplexity / research platform lane
- Azure Secure Orchestration as a future secure-path architecture lane

## License and runtime policy

Vendor licenses, subscriptions and API usage are customer-owned, pass-through, prepaid or capped unless an executed order form explicitly states otherwise.

Atlas prices the solution layer unless a package explicitly includes a defined runtime budget or license pass-through item.

## Data-handling note

Data handling, retention, logging, training-policy controls and administrative functionality vary by provider, plan, account type, configuration and delivery mode. Public copy must not generalize one provider's controls across all platform lanes.

## Public wording controls

Avoid:

- official OpenAI partner
- Atlas GPT
- ChatGPT Atlas
- guaranteed accuracy
- guaranteed private AI
- fully secure unless validated controls exist
- vendor names in product titles that imply ownership or endorsement

Use:

- platform lane
- may run on
- configured for
- customer-owned or pass-through license
- human review required
- architecture review required for confidential or control-sensitive workflows

This document is a governance draft and must receive legal/commercial review before being treated as approved public copy.
