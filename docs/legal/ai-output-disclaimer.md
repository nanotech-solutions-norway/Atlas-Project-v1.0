# Atlas AI Output Disclaimer

Status: Draft for legal/commercial review
Date: 2026-06-08
Owner system: GitHub
Related issue: #4

## Purpose

This document controls public disclaimer wording for Atlas AI website pages, Trust Center content, onboarding forms, pricing pages and package pages.

## Core disclaimer

AI-generated outputs can be incomplete, outdated, inaccurate or unsuitable for a specific use case. Atlas AI packages are designed to improve workflow structure and productivity, but outputs must be reviewed by a qualified human before reliance, publication, submission or operational use.

## Professional-use boundary

Atlas AI does not replace licensed professional advice, statutory review, legal counsel, accounting sign-off, medical/healthcare decision-making, financial advice or regulated professional judgment.

## Student-use boundary

Student packages support planning, explanation, revision and responsible source use. They must not be used for cheating, ghostwriting, hidden answer generation, direct assignment submission or academic-integrity bypass.

## Consumer-use boundary

Consumer packages support everyday productivity, planning and writing. They are not emergency tools, emotional companions, mental-health services, medical tools, financial advisors or legal advisors.

## Secure-data boundary

Ordinary website forms are for low-risk qualification only. Confidential, restricted, regulated or customer-sensitive material must not be submitted through public forms. Sensitive material is collected only through a controlled secure path after qualification when such path is approved and available.

## Review rule

This disclaimer is a governance draft and must receive legal/commercial review before being treated as approved public copy.
